Our Limitations

Ʊ

There is no ground truth available to objectively verify whether the retrieved papers are truly the most relevant

for a given query.

Ʊ

The LLM Judge cannot guarantee factual accuracy of summaries, as it relies solely on the model's own

understanding of the paper.

Ʊ

The pipeline depends on a single language model for both summarization and evaluation, which may introduce

some bias or blind spots across all outputs.
